IIM Certificate Program Fees India 2026 — Complete Comparison

IIM certificate fees — the full range in 2026

IIM certificate program fees in India range from approximately ₹2L (shorter 6-month certificates) to ₹8–12L (IIM Nagpur Blended MBA — full 2-year degree program). This guide covers every program with verified fee ranges. Note: fees are exclusive of GST (18%) and are subject to change — verify directly with enrollment partner before committing.

Programs under ₹2.5L + GST (entry-level IIM certs)

IIM Kozhikode Analytics & GenAI (~₹2L + GST, 12 months) — Best value IIM certificate for business professionals. No coding. NIRF #3 institution. IIM Trichy Banking & Finance (~₹2L + GST, 12 months). IIM Mumbai Corporate Finance (~₹2L + GST, 6 months).

Programs ₹2.5–3.5L + GST (mid-range)

IIM Kozhikode New Age Management (~₹2–3L + GST). IIM Nagpur FinTech (~₹2–3L + GST, 1 year). IIM Nagpur Cyber + DS (~₹2–3L + GST).

Programs ₹3L+ + GST (premium IIM certs)

IIM Ahmedabad FRCG (~₹3–4L + GST) — worth it for CAs, CS and finance leaders. IIM Ahmedabad AGMP (~₹3.5–4L + GST). IIM Indore AI & Cyber (~₹3–4L + GST).

EMI options and payment structure

Most IIM certificate programs through our enrollment partner offer 0% EMI for qualified applicants. Typical structure: application fee (₹3,000–10,000) + program fee in 2–4 instalments. EMI options range from 6 to 24 months. Free counselling to understand full fee structure and EMI eligibility — talk to our advisor.

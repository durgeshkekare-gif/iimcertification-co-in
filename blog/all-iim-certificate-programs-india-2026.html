All IIM Certificate Programs India 2026 — Complete List Across Every IIM

IIM certificates — the complete picture in 2026

India has 20 IIMs established under the IIM Act of Parliament. Not all offer executive certificate programs for working professionals — those that do are covered in this guide. The programs span finance, analytics, AI, cybersecurity, management, banking, FinTech and blockchain. Fees range from ₹2L (short certificates) to ₹8–12L (full blended MBA). Duration ranges from 4 months (IIM Ahmedabad AGMP) to 2 years (IIM Nagpur Blended MBA).

Top-ranked IIM programs by NIRF

IIM Ahmedabad (NIRF #1): FRCG (finance governance), Financial Technologies & Analytics, AGMP (general management). India's most prestigious management institution — AACSB and EQUIS accredited.

IIM Kozhikode (NIRF #3): Analytics & GenAI, Investment Banking, New Age Management. Triple-Crown accredited (AACSB, AMBA, EQUIS). FT-ranked #69 globally.

IIM Indore (NIRF #7): AI & Cybersecurity, VUCA Leadership. Triple-Crown accredited. Unique AI+Cyber combination program.

Specialised IIM programs — not covered at every IIM

Investment banking at IIM level: only IIM Kozhikode. Ethical hacking and cyber: IIM Indore (management) and IIM Nagpur (cyber+DS combo). Full MBA degree for working professionals: only IIM Nagpur Blended MBA. Blockchain: IIM Visakhapatnam. Banking & Finance: IIM Trichy. FinTech: IIM Nagpur and IIM Ahmedabad.

How to choose from 20 IIM programs

The most important filter is specialisation — choose based on your career target, not institution rank alone. A finance professional should prioritise IIM Ahmedabad FRCG or IIM Trichy Banking & Finance over a generic management program from a higher-ranked IIM.
